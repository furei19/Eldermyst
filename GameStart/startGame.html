In a realm distant from our own, where diverse creatures and enigmatic entities once lived in harmony, a transformative shift began to unravel the delicate balance. As the powerful seized control and the cries of the defenseless echoed through the land, an unsettling chaos began to unfurl its dark wings. It was in the midst of this tumultuous upheaval that the world fractured, splitting into three distinct realms, each bearing its own mystique and allure. The first, known as Alterra, nestled in the depths, whispered secrets of forgotten mysteries. Above it lay Arcanterra, the middle realm, where enigmas intertwined with reality in an intricate dance. And high above them all, beyond the ethereal veil, stretched Aetheris, a realm of boundless wonder and untamed dreams. Thus, the tale of these interconnected realms was set into motion, where destinies entwined and the very fabric of existence hung in a delicate balance.
